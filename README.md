# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard developed as part of the **Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship – Task 2 (2026)**. This project analyses customer churn and retention patterns using the Telco Customer Churn dataset to help businesses understand why customers leave and identify strategies to improve customer retention.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the most important drivers of business growth, especially for subscription-based companies. This project focuses on analyzing customer demographics, service usage, contracts, and billing information to uncover the factors influencing customer churn.

The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs) that enable stakeholders to identify high-risk customer segments and make informed business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The primary objectives of this project were to:

- Clean and prepare customer data for analysis.
- Calculate key customer retention and churn metrics.
- Identify customer segments with the highest churn.
- Analyze customer lifetime patterns using tenure cohorts.
- Determine the main drivers of customer churn.
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer information including:

- Customer demographics
- Subscription details
- Contract types
- Internet services
- Payment methods
- Monthly and total charges
- Customer tenure
- Churn status

Each row represents a unique customer.

## 📈 Key Insights

The analysis revealed several important business insights:

- Customers on **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn rate.
- Customers within their **first 12 months** are significantly more likely to churn.
- **Fiber optic** customers experience higher churn compared to DSL customers.
- Customers using **Electronic Check** have the highest churn among payment methods.
- Approximately **73% of customers remain active**, while **26.5% have churned**.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are proposed:

- Encourage customers to switch from month-to-month contracts to longer-term contracts through discounts or loyalty incentives.
- Improve customer engagement during the first year of subscription, when churn risk is highest.
- Investigate service quality and pricing for Fiber Optic customers to understand higher churn levels.
- Promote additional services such as Tech Support and Online Security to improve customer retention.
- Develop proactive retention campaigns targeting customers identified as high-risk.
